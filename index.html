About me

Hi there. I'm James, and I'm a second year Software Engineering student studying at the University of Technology Sydney. I have a demonstrable passion for programming and everything software related, having spent countless hours on self-study and working on personal projects.

I technically started programming when I was around ten years old, messing around with Java and OpenGL but not really understanding a thing I was doing, and eventually giving up. About two or three years later, however, I started programming small games in GameMaker Studio and ended up designing and developing some websites. I consider my programming skills to have grown somewhat exponentially over the past one or two years in particular, and have since created substantially more complex tech demos, games, and software.
